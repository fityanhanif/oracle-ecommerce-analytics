Business interpretation 4.75x

Late orders are far more likely to receive poor reviews.

The low-review rate rises from 11.38% to 54.02% when delivery is late. This makes logistics reliability the clearest first lever for customer satisfaction.

Revenue needs quality controls.

High-revenue sellers can still be operationally risky. Seller scorecards should combine revenue, late rate, and average review score.

Repeat customers are scarce but valuable.

Repeat customers represent a smaller group, but their average monetary value is nearly double the one-time customer group.

Credit card dominates payment value.

Credit card payments account for the largest payment value and average 3.51 installments.

Category focus is clear.

Health and beauty, watches and gifts, home goods, sports, and electronics accessories drive top category revenue.
